Rebuilding Trust, Access, and Intelligence in Residential Real Estate

The Problem We’re Solving

Residential real estate, especially in markets like Bangalore, suffers from three structural issues:

Fragmented Supply

Property inventory lives in thousands of WhatsApp groups, personal spreadsheets, and broker phones. There is no trusted, searchable, up-to-date source of real supply.

Broken Trust

Brokers often don’t trust each other. Buyers don’t trust brokers. This leads to duplicated listings, poor customer experiences, and high friction in transactions.

Poor Buyer Experience

Consumer portals optimize for ads and lead generation, not outcomes. Buyers are overwhelmed with irrelevant listings and forced to talk to multiple agents to piece together the truth.

The result: inefficiency, opacity, and frustration for everyone involved.
Our Approach: Start with the Broker, Not the Portal.
We believe the only way to fix the consumer experience is to first fix the broker ecosystem.

What is RealBroker (RB)?

RealBroker.app is an invite-only, broker-to-broker network designed to enable trusted collaboration and structured inventory sharing among high-quality real estate professionals.

RB helps brokers:

  • Share verified, live inventory in a structured format
  • Collaborate on deals with clear ownership and trust
  • Reduce dependency on spam-heavy WhatsApp groups
  • Access better demand without racing to the bottom on commissions

RB is not a marketplace for everyone — it is curated by design. Quality, reputation, and behavior matter.

What is Propalyst?

Propalyst.com is the consumer-facing search and discovery platform powered by RealBroker’s network. Instead of scraping the internet or selling leads:

  • Propalyst surfaces real, broker-verified inventory
  • Buyers engage through a conversational, human-assisted search experience
  • Matching is driven by local broker intelligence, not just filters

Propalyst also plays the role of a buyer’s advisor / buyer-side broker:

  • We help buyers clarify requirements, shortlist options, and coordinate viewings
  • We are the online concierge & search partner while Viewings & Transactions will be fulfilled through our partner brokers from the RealBroker network
  • Revenue is generated by participating in the buyer-side brokerage fee, sharing a portion of the commission earned on successful transactions

How RealBroker Enables Propalyst

RealBroker is the engine. Propalyst is the interface.

  • RB provides clean, trusted, live supply
  • RB ensures accountability and data quality
  • Propalyst converts that supply into a high-trust buyer experience

This creates a powerful flywheel: Better brokers → better inventory → better buyers → more trust → stronger network

Why This Matters

Real estate is a relationship-driven, local, trust-based business — and technology has largely ignored that reality. RealBroker + Propalyst are designed to:

  • Elevate the role of the professional broker
  • Bring transparency and intelligence to transactions
  • Deliver a materially better experience for buyers and sellers
